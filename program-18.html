ESL -Adult Learning

This course primarily focus on the fundamental concepts of the English language (Ex. vocabulary, grammar, spelling, verb tense, and basic civics lessons) as well as life skills of American culture. Students are expected to practice newly acquired English skills at home. Students receive instruction and practice in the areas of reading, writing, listening, and speaking. Some benefits of ESL learning are:

  • More professional/job opportunities
  • Access to academic opportunities
  • For your family life
  • For your social life
