WHY THP?

14 Reasons Why THP is Your Best Choice!

You only want to learn what you need and you want to learn it fast! We get that. Here are fourteen ways THP prepares its students for real world success:

1. Low Cost Programs

Our programs start at $500! Let's face it, you DO NOT need to spend $10,000 to $50,000 to get a quality education. Who wants to still be paying off student loans at 65 years old?

2. Short Term Classes

6 weeks and we'll give you a career. We know you only want to learn what you need. We get that.

3. Hands-On Training

You'll learn by doing; using professional techniques and tools on the latest software.

4. Small Work Teams

No student willing to learn gets left behind. You'll get personal, one-on-one training to make sure you understand each skill as it is meant to be applied.

5. Flexible Class Scheduling

Day and evening classes let you work full-time. Programs and schedules vary by course interest.

6. Experienced Professionals

Your classes are taught by instructors with real-world experience. They'll give you the inside scoop to help you be successful in your chosen career.

7. Modern Equipment

You'll get trained on the kind of tools and equipment that healthcare professionals are using now!

8. No Credit Check Loans

We'll help you uncover sources for no credit check loans to pay for your education. Loans are available for those who qualify.

9. Easy Payment Plans

Payment plans are available for those who qualify.

10. Job Placement Assistance

A dedicated member of our staff will assist in helping you put together job-winning resumes, getting ready for interviews and finding full-time or part-time employment opportunities

11. Timely Program Offerings

You'll get trained in programs that are designed to prepare you for employment in in-demand careers.

12. Learning Centers

You can get extra help from instructors to help you keep up or to get on the fast-track.

13. Online Courses

For your convenience, we offer several courses online. Check with our program director for details. Not all programs are available online.

14. Right-to-Act

Giving our instructors the "Right to Act" means your concerns can be responded to in minutes instead of the weeks the TV schools take. We are confident our instructors will do what is in the best interest of the student while remembering to stay within certain ethical guidelines.
